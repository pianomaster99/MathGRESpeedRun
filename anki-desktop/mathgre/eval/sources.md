# Named sources for GRE Math content & verification

Every AI output in this project traces to one of these named, authoritative
sources. Used to (a) ground the gold set, (b) generate cards, and (c) let the
card-checker cite a basis for each verdict.

## Official (ETS)
- **GRE Mathematics Test practice book (GR0568)** — ETS. Official retired exam +
  content outline. https://www.ets.org/pdfs/gre/practice-book-math.pdf
- **GR1268 / GR1768** — ETS retired forms (identical questions). Mirrors:
  https://tutoring.neaton.org/materials/math_gre.php
- **GRE Subject Test content & structure** — ETS (Calculus 50%, Algebra 25%,
  Additional 25%). https://www.ets.org/gre/test-takers/subject-tests/about/content-structure.html

## Open textbooks (per topic, all openly licensed / CC)
- **Calculus** — OpenStax *Calculus* Vol. 1–3 (CC BY-NC-SA). https://openstax.org/details/books/calculus-volume-1
- **Linear algebra** — David Austin, *Understanding Linear Algebra* (CC BY);
  Jim Hefferon, *Linear Algebra*. https://understandinglinearalgebra.org
- **Abstract algebra & number theory** — Thomas Judson, *Abstract Algebra:
  Theory and Applications* (GFDL/CC). http://abstract.ups.edu
- **Real analysis** — Jiří Lebl, *Basic Analysis: Introduction to Real Analysis*;
  William F. Trench, *Introduction to Real Analysis*. https://www.jirka.org/ra/
- **Topology** — standard undergraduate topology (Munkres-level definitions);
  general-topology definitions per ETS "Additional Topics".
- **Complex analysis** — standard undergraduate complex variables (Cauchy-Riemann,
  Cauchy's theorem/formula, residues).
- **Probability & statistics** — standard undergraduate probability (Kolmogorov
  axioms, common distributions).
- **Discrete math / combinatorics** — Kenneth Rosen, *Discrete Mathematics and
  Its Applications* (topic reference); Levin, *Discrete Mathematics: An Open
  Introduction* (open). https://discrete.openmathbooks.org
- **Numerical analysis** — standard methods (Newton, bisection, trapezoid/Simpson).

## Practice problems (style/difficulty reference)

- mathsub.com solutions to GR0568 / GR1768. https://www.mathsub.com/solutions/
- AIM Open Textbook Initiative (approved list). https://textbooks.aimath.org/textbooks/approved-textbooks/
